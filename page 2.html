Reason Two

Throughout the series, victim blaming was present everywhere. Victim blaming is putting fault on the victim and in a way telling them that they're to blame for what's happened to them.

Several times, people spoke about her and how she should've told someone about how she was feeling, making it her fault for not getting help. Victim blaming is unhealthy to all victims, closing them off more.
